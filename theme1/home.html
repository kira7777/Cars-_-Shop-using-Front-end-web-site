About safety department

Advance Trac® with Roll Stability Control. This safety system uses gyroscopic sensors to monitor the front-to-back orientation and the roll rate of the vehicle. This allows for better control in adverse road conditions and when the system detects under- or over-steering. Curve Control. This is part of the Advance Trac® and RSC system that detects when the vehicle is going into a curve at too great a rate of speed. If the system detects that the vehicle is going too fast for a curve, it can slow it down by up to 10 miles per hour. Blind Spot Information System (BLIS). This system uses multiple-beam radar sensors in the rear quarter-panels to detect traffic as it enters the vehicle’s blind spot. An indicator light on the corresponding side-view mirror will illuminate if there is a hazard in either the left or right side blind spot.
